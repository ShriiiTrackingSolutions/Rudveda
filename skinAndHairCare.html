Ayurvedic ubtan is a mixture of different herbal powders that have beneficial effects on skin. The herbal powder can be mixed with yogurt, herbal extract or other natural potions to get a creamy paste that is applied all over body. Ubtan works as an Ayurvedic Body Polish and Body Wrap which is mainly intended for exfoliation and rejuvenation of skin. Some Ubtans are also made from herbs that have antibacterial or antifungal properties.

What to expect

Ubtan involves a full body Abhyangam massage followed by herbal paste application all over the body.

Benefits of Ubtan

Ayurvedic Ubtan therapy improves lustre of the skin by improving its health. It also has antifungal, antibacterial, antiperspirant, deodorant & skin-toning properties.

How it works

It maintains PH balance of the skin and opens up blocked pores.

Not recommended (contraindicated) in case of

Psoriasis or skin infection.

Soundarya is an Ayurvedic skin care treatment.

What to expect

Soundarya is a classical combination of a full body Ayurvedic Abhyanga massage that is followed by application of Ayurvedic mask containing paste of skin-nourishing fruit supplements.

Benefits of Soundarya

This Ayurvedic therapy for skin softens and reduces scars and provides good complexion to the skin. The mask detoxifies and nourishes the skin leaving it smooth and silky.

How it works

The nutrients from the herbs and the fruit extracts nourish the skin, open up blocked pores and help exfoliation.

Not recommended (contraindicated) in case of

Psoriasis or skin infection.

Shringara is a traditional beauty package.

What to expect

Shringara begins with a full body Abhyanga massage and scrub. It is then followed by an Ayurvedic paste application. The body is wrapped with long banana leaves for long lasting effect.

Benefits of Shringara

It improves skin health and enhances the glow and shine all over the body as a result. Regular sittings help to improve skin texture.

How it works

Abhyangam and the scrub exfoliate the skin. Traditional herbs like Turmeric and Tulsi cleans the body and help improve the skin texture.

Not recommended (contraindicated) in case of

Skin infection or injury.

Ayurvedic Udvartanam is a fat burning full body massage for Weight Loss. This Ayurvedic full body massage uses some special strokes especially at the areas of fat deposition in the body. The massage helps to melt the fat which is further expelled out from body through therapies such as Kashaya Vasti.

Udvartanam is indicated in following health conditions

  • Weight Loss
  • Skin & Hair Care

What to expect

Udvartanam is a massage that uses herbal powder mixed with warm oil. This paste is massaged on to the whole body with special attention to areas of increased fat deposits.

Benefits of Udvartanam

Ayurvedic Udvartanam treatment is effective in cases of obesity, hemiplegia, paralysis, skin diseases, and impaired circulation.

How it works

The massage strokes help to melt the fat by creating friction and remove by-products through the excretory system.

Not recommended (contraindicated) in case of

Eczema and Psoriasis.

Thalapothichil includes application of Ayurvedic paste containing some herbs. What to expect

Thalapothichil is a gentle head massage with amla, curd and triphala paste application.

How it works

The herbs in the paste penetrate into the brain cells. The healing properties of the herbs also calm down the nervous system.

Not recommended (contraindicated) in case of

Head injury.
