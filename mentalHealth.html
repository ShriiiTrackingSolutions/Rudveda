Mental Health

Introduction to Mental Wellness Through Yoga

Discover how the ancient practice of yoga can be a powerful tool for modern mental health. By harmonizing body and mind, yoga supports emotional resilience, inner peace, and stress management.

Mental Health Benefits of Yoga

  • Reduces Stress & Anxiety – Yoga lowers cortisol levels, calming the nervous system.
  • Improves Mood – Boosts serotonin and dopamine, reducing symptoms of depression.
  • Enhances Mental Clarity – Supports better concentration, memory, and emotional balance.

What Science Says

Clinical studies confirm that yoga activates brain areas linked to relaxation and emotional control. It also reduces inflammation and improves heart-rate variability — both vital for mental well-being.

Types of Yoga for Mental Health

Awakens energy and enhances mental clarity.

Ideal for deep relaxation and stress relief.

Yin Yoga

Great for emotional release and inner peace.
